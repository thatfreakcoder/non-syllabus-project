What We Offer

Weekly Timetable

It will provide us links of daily going classes, from monday to saturday. Classes conduct on google meet. Student can easily find link of every respective class. Timetable will update weekly by class tutor.

Semester Wise Syllabus

Syllabus will be shown according to students respective year (odd and even semester). Will be helpful for every year student. Will be easy to find syllabus according to their semester. Syllabus is verified by its respective subject teacher.

Request or Upload Notes

Student can request notes which they need. Notes will be uploaded by teachers. Student can also upload notes but it will be verified by teachers. Every student of each semester can request for any notes related to any subject.
